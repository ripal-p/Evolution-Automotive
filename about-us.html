About Us

OUR HISTORY

Experience & Great Services 

 

Evolution Automotive Services is a family owned and operated business by Ivan Colaco. Evolution Automotive has been providing expert vehicle repairs for clients in the Eight Mile Plains area area since 1984. And in its present location since 1992. For your convenience we are located on the corner of Warrigal and Padstow roads in the BP service station facility.

Ivan’s no nonsense approach to clients safety ensures all cars leave his workshop in the best possible operating condition, his instruction to his staff is “treat the customer like they are family”, his client base is testament to his customer satisfaction.

Evolution Automotive Service are able to accept all credit & debit cards for service or repairs. They are custom fleet approved as with most other novated lease suppliers.

Give Ivan and his staff a call to discuss what they can do for you.
