Cooling Systems & Radiators

The job of your radiator & other cooling system components is to keep coolant flowing throughout your engine, keeping the engine at the desired operating temperature. If there’s a blockage or a leak in the cooling system, your engine can quickly overheat and your vehicle could break down & this can be extremely expensive to rectify. We recommend having your cooling system professionally inspected by Evolution Automotive Services at least once a year. We are cooling system experts and we know how to keep the coolant flowing, and your car going.
