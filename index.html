Evolution Automotive Service Guarantee

We promise to our clients that we will always provide you with guaranteed workmanship, quality replacement parts and quality lubricants.

We always provide accurate quotes, a courtesy call if further repairs are needed (before proceeding), clear and helpful explanations of all repairs required and no surprises when we hand you the invoice.

For our business to continue to survive, it is important to us that our clients are happy with the experience of dealing with Evolution Automotive Services, knowing that they have been treated with courtesy and respect.

We will not participate in the practice of “Discount service advertising” to get clients through the door only to “up sell” you extra work on your vehicle. (Unfortunately this is becoming an increasing trend in all areas of the automotive industry).

It is our belief that “satisfying our clients’ needs” by using quality parts/ lubricants and doing the job the correct way, is the best way to build relationships with our clients.

We will always provide you with an honest appraisal of your vehicles condition to ensure you are fully aware of repairs required and costs involved. “We do not proceed without your approval”

Whilst we appreciate that times are tough and money must be spent wisely, our clients’ safety and satisfaction is our first concern.

We are committed to building a long term relationship with you and providing you with excellent service at the best possible price, without utilising dishonest or misleading advertising.

Evolution Automotive Services Proprietor
